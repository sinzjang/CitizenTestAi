# Release Notes - Version 1.0.8

## 🎉 What's New

### Daily Study Reminders
- **Set custom study reminders** to stay on track with your citizenship test preparation
- Choose specific days of the week (weekdays, weekends, or custom schedule)
- Set multiple reminder times throughout the day
- Receive notifications at your preferred study times

### Improvements
- Enhanced notification system with reliable scheduling
- Better performance with optimized logging
- Improved app stability and bug fixes

## 📱 How to Use Reminders

1. Go to **Study Calendar** screen
2. Tap the notification bell icon
3. Enable **Daily Reminders**
4. Set your preferred study times and days
5. Tap **Save Reminders**

Stay consistent with your study routine and ace your citizenship interview! 🇺🇸
