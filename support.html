❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using the AI mock interview?

Go to the main menu and select "AI Mock Interview". Make sure to allow microphone permissions when prompted.

The app is not recognizing my voice properly. What should I do?

Ensure you're in a quiet environment and speak clearly. Check that your device's microphone is working properly in other apps.

Can I change the language of the app?

Yes! Go to Settings and select your preferred language from 9 available options.

How do I access my progress reports?

Tap on "My Progress" in the main menu to view detailed analytics of your learning journey.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all your personal information and learning data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties.
